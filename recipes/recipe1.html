Background

These chocolate chip cookies are the best. They're easy to make and taste amazing, they are sure to bring a smile to your face.

The Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar, packed
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (14 oz)

Instructions

Step-by-Step Instructions
1 Preheat oven to 375 F. Line 3 baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
2 Mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl. Set aside.
3 Cream together butter and sugars until combined.
4 Beat in eggs and vanilla until light (about 1 minute).
5 Mix dry ingredients until combined.
6 Add chocolate chips and mix well.
7 Roll 2-3 tbsp of dough at a time into balls & place on prepared cookie sheets.
8 Bake for 8-10 minutes. Take them out when they are just barely starting to turn brown.
9 Let sit on baking pan for 5 minutes before removing to cooling rack.
