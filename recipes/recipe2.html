Background

This cornbread is super easy to make and goes perfectly with other foods

The Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flower
  • 1 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg

Instructions

1 Gather ingredients.
2 Preheat oven to 400 F. Lightly grease a 9-inch round cake pan.
3 Whisk flour, cornmeal, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl.
4 Add milk, vegetable oil, and egg; whisk until well combined.
5 Pour batter into the prepared pan
6 Bake in preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the pan comes out clean, 20-25 minutes.
7 Enjoy!
